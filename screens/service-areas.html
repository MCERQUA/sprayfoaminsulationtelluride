Why Local Expertise Matters

High-altitude insulation isn't just about R-value; it's about managing extreme vapor drive and dramatic temperature swings. At 8,000+ feet, the air density and moisture behavior differ significantly from sea-level applications.

Our team understands the specific requirements for San Miguel County building codes and the unique thermal bridges found in mountain modern architecture. We use specialized foam formulations that maintain their cell integrity and insulating properties in the thin, cold air of the San Juan Mountains.

  • check_circle Snow-load thermal bridge mitigation
  • check_circle High-altitude vapor barrier strategies
